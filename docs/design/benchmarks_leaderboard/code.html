MMLU

Massive Multitask Language Understanding. A benchmark designed to measure knowledge acquired during pretraining by evaluating models across 57 subjects across STEM, the humanities, the social sciences, and more.

GSM8K

Grade School Math 8K. High-quality grade school math word problems. It tests the model's ability to perform multi-step mathematical reasoning.

HumanEval

Released by OpenAI, this dataset measures functional correctness for synthesizing programs from docstrings. It solves problems in Python.
